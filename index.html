James Galt is a French photographer and software developer. By day an application security engineer; evenings and weekends, he builds software and makes pictures. He shoots a Hasselblad X2D, mostly in HDR, and has spent years photographing wildlife, landscapes, and people across France, Japan, Zanzibar, and Tanzania.
